The Generation Whisky

The Generation Premium Blended Whisky is a Smooth Full-bodied Blend of the finest imported Scotch Malts matured and preserved in Handpicked Oak casks and carefully Selected Premium Indian Grain Spirits. This vibrant brand’s blend reflects the passion of the master blender to craft a full bodied premium whisky having well balanced malt, woody aroma and character with lingering, mellow taste.

Launched to the Indian markets in March 2016 after years of research and masterful blending, it is ADS Sprits most premium blend, at par with global brands on quality. The Generation has been priced extremely competitively to become the regular go to brand for connoisseur of premium whisky

Read More
Tasting Notes

Initial nose of Roasted Barley and Cereals with unique sweetness and hint of fresh Oak. Medium bodied with rich notes of Malt, vanilla, stewed fruits & finally resins and prunes. The blend lends a fresh finish with lots of depth, soft and fragrant aromas with a contrast of sweet notes of Vanilla, Honey and mellow Woody palate finish.

Long lingering finish with Peat and oak till end.

ROYAL WHISKY SOUR

Depending on who you believe, the mojito either came from the Spanish word ‘mojar’, which means to wet, or the African word ‘mojo’, which means to cast a spell. Anybody who’s ever tasted one will agree that it’s thirst quenching and spellbinding in equal measures.

WHAT YOU'LL NEED

60 ml Royal Green Whisky, 22 ml lemon juice, 22 ml simple syrup, Dash of grenadine, Orange slice and maraschino cherry for garnish

STEPS
  • Pour the ingredients into a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  • train into a chilled cocktail glass or a collins glass filled with ice.
  • Garnish with a cherry and orange slice flag.
