Infrastructure

State of Art distillery equipped with most modern technology, wherein highest degree of quality is maintained by our own teams

Distillery & Bottling plants

ADS group comprises of three distilleries, ADS Sprits Pvt. Ltd located in Beri, Jhajjar Haryana, ADS Agro Reengus, Sikar, Rajasthan and ADS Distillery in Sikandrabad, Bulandsahar, Uttar Pradesh. From the current 105 KL per day production capacity, we are moving soon to a 230 KL per day capacity. All three Units having Own Steam Power Generation Unit. Our main unit, ADS Spirits, is situated in Haryana a 25-acre (10.0 ha) site in Beri. A state of Art distillery and equipped with most modern technology like seven columns Distillation, Evaporation, Zero Liquid Discharge Unit, two type of DDG Dryer (Tube Bundle and Flash Ring Dryer) The distillery was built in 2011 and commenced commercial Production from 2013. ADS also has contract bottling across the country, wherein highest degree of quality is maintained by our own teams. ADS procures high quality grains of different types from the food bastion heartland of north and central India to produce the finest spirit for the manufacture of its brands along with the finest Vatted Aged Malts from Scotland.

Our grain spirits are of the highest quality and we have global and domestic giants like Pernod Ricard, Diageo & Radico Khaitan etc as our valued customers.

Some of USP of Distillery

Its First Distillery in India who started Using Liquor Ammonia in Place of Common Practice Urea/DAP in other Unit. We Proudly say many of Distillery now shifting on Liquor Ammonia

We are the only Distillery in India who is having Two type of Dryer for DDGS to achieved a good quality of DDGS

We are only Unit in India who is having Bag filter and ESP on Power Generation Unit to achieved Zero AIR Pollution

We are first Unit in India who achieved 16% Alcohol in Fermentation and Produced Potable Spirits

As a part of innovation, we are first Distillers who have achieved Fermenters and Pre Fermenters in Stainless steel.

As a part of our CSR responsibility we believe in carbon credits and have been collecting Co2 from in all our plant from inception and hence have reduced the carbon foot print.

Quality control measures

Our quality standards are in line with internationally recognised norms. Modern equipment, amenities and proper facilities along with highly trained staff adds to adoption and implementation of stringent quality measures across all facets of raw material, packaging material and finished goods.

Proper storage and adoption of ever evolving global norms help us in roll out of the finest products.
